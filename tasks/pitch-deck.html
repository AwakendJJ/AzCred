How It Works

Three steps to on-chain credit

Step 1
Register Identity
Agent gets an ERC-8004 identity NFT on Creditcoin via the Identity Registry.
Step 2
Build Reputation
On-chain feedback signals (success rate, uptime, performance) accumulate in the Reputation Registry.
Step 3
Get Credit
AzCred reads the reputation, computes a score, and assigns a tiered CTC credit line — instantly.
Credit Tiers
Tier 1  Score 0–33 100 tCTC
Tier 2  Score 34–66 500 tCTC
Tier 3  Score 67–100 1,000 tCTC
SCORING FORMULA
score = successRate × 0.40
       + uptime     × 0.35
       + starred    × 0.25

Live Demo Flow

End-to-end in 5 steps

01
Connect Wallet
Connect to Creditcoin Testnet via MetaMask or any EVM wallet through RainbowKit.
02
View Agents
Dashboard loads all agents you own from the IdentityRegistry, with reputation signals.
03
Assign Credit
Contract reads ERC-8004 reputation, computes score, issues tiered credit line on-chain.
04
Draw tCTC
Agent draws CTC from the protocol pool up to its credit limit. Funds transfer instantly.
05
Repay
Agent repays tCTC (full or partial). Outstanding balance decreases. Credit freed up.
